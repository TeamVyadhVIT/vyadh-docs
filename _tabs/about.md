## Purpose

This site serves as the master documentation repository for Team Vyadh.

The goal is simple:
- Preserve knowledge long term
- Track engineering progress
- Ensure no R&D effort is lost between batches

---

## What Goes Here

Everything we work on, including:

- Research and background studies  
- Design decisions and system architecture  
- Experiments and prototypes  
- Issues encountered and how they were resolved  
- What worked, what didnt, and why  
- Final implementation and outcome

From initial idea to final testing, the full engineering lifecycle should be documented here.

---

## Why It Matters

Without structured documentation:
- Knowledge is lost  
- Mistakes are repeated  
- Progress becomes hard to measure  

This repository ensures continuity, accountability, and long term growth.

TLDR: If it was built, tested, improved, or deployed, it should be documented here.
